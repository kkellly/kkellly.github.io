Year | 2020

Brand Name & Logo

“bare”
baresilk emphasizes on the idea of embracing your bare face, and enhancing the natural features instead of completely covering them with cosmetics. “bare” also expresses the use of nude colours, and minimal ingredients in our products.

“silk”
Silk is a semi-opaque fabric that is glossy on both sides, which illustrates our concept — our products are tools to enhance your natural beauty. Silk also represents the smooth & glossy finishes of our products.

The logo expresses the 3 major aspects of the brand — beauty, inclusiveness and the semi-opaque nature of silk.
