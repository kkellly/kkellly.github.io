Characters Relationship

characters’ relationships in Star Wars. Including:

1. Masters & Apprentice
2. Friendship
3. Original Droid Owners
4. Romance
5. Family
6. Year Of Death
7. Battle Significance

Elements like lightsabers are also used to illustrate the significance
of major battles between characters, enhancing the overall Star Wars concept.
